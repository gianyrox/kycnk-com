What is KYC No Knowledge?

KYC No Knowledge (KYCnk) provides a decentralized solution to the traditional "Know Your Customer" process. With KYCnk, we ensure that your personal data is never exposed to third parties, while still providing proof of your identity through zero-knowledge proofs.

By using a Solana wallet account hash, we can verify your customer data and allow other companies to access only the necessary, verifiable information—without compromising your privacy.

How It Works

  1. Create Your Solana Wallet: Link your Solana wallet to our system.
  2. Verify with Zero Knowledge Proofs: Use zero-knowledge proofs to verify your identity.
  3. Grant Permissions: Control which companies have access to what information without sharing sensitive data.
